Our Farming Story

Founded in 2010, Green Valley Farms began with a simple mission: to grow food the way nature intended.

Today, we operate 200 acres of certified organic farmland, providing fresh produce to local communities while maintaining our commitment to sustainable practices.

Our team of passionate farmers works tirelessly to bring you the highest quality fruits, vegetables, and dairy products while preserving the land for future generations.
